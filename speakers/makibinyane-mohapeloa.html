About Makibinyane

Makibinyane Mohapeloa is a professional entrepreneur who has successfully launched multiple businesses. He was named as one of the top 30 young entrepreneurs by the Tshwane Economic Development Agency. Makibinyane is passionate about the growth potential of the African continent as well as the potential role of young people in Africa’s economy.

A BCom Marketing Management graduate, Mohapeloa is also a graduate of the Social Entrepreneurship Programme at the Gordon Institute of Business Science (GIBS), as well as the B-BBEE Management programme with the Unisa Graduate School of Business Leadership (SBL) and Wits Enterprise.
