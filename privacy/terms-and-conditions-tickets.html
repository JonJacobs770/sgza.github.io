Event Ticket Terms and Conditions

Event Cancellation Policy

Although we hope it never happens, there may be certain reasons beyond our control, for which, we may need to cancel or postpone one or more events. In such situations, we will notify you as far ahead of time as possible, so you can plan accordingly.

  • In case of cancellation by SUGSA, we will refund the full conference or event fees. Refunds will be issued within 21 business days after the scheduled conclusion of the event.
  • In case the conference or event is postponed, we won't be able to offer a refund. If you are not able to attend the event on the new dates, you can either nominate another person or use the funds towards any future events.

We apologize that SUGSA cannot, and does not, make any exceptions to this policy.

Kindly note that we will not be liable for any losses or damages arising as a consequence of such cancellation(s).

Attendee Cancellation Policy

If you are unable to attend an event, a replacement delegate may be sent in your place at no extra charge. Notification must be made in writing and received to info@sugsa.org.za prior to the event commencing.

Any cancellation must be made in writing and sent by email to info@sugsa.org.za.

If booking within 7 days of the event start date, full payment is required and due at time of booking. No refund will be provided for bookings/registrations made within 7 days of the event.

Sharing of Tickets

SUGSA does not allow shared tickets. Each ticket allows admittance of one person only.

Change of Program Content, Format and/or Event Timing

SUGSA will endeavour to ensure that conference programs are correct at the time of the event. Unfortunately, we may need to alter the program prior to or during the event and reserve the right to do so without notice. We also reserve the right to change the format of the event or postpone the event (where no refund will be issued) or cancel the event (when full refunds will be issued). SUGSA will make available course documentation to any delegate who is unable to attend and who has paid in full. This includes on-demand access to our online-only events for 12 months. Unfortunately, some speakers request that their content is not distributed. SUGSA is not responsible for any loss or damage as a result of a substitution, alteration, format change, postponement or cancellation of an event.

Please note: not all speakers choose to provide their content or presentations.

Group Bookings

In order to receive a Group Booking Discount, all members of the group must come from the same organisation (or be included on the one invoice), be registered at the same time and registration must be finalised in a single payment.

Discounts

Individual discounts or Group Booking discounts will only be applied to the full ticket price and can not be combined with other discounts.

Payment

For individual and group attendee bookings, payment is required either at the time of booking or within 7 days of an invoice being issued. No admittance will be granted where payment has not been received in full unless a prior arrangement has been made in writing with SUGSA. If booking within 7 days of the event start date, full payment is required and due at the time of booking.

Contact by 3rd Party Notice

SUGSA does not share our attendee lists to partners and/or sponsors of specific events.
