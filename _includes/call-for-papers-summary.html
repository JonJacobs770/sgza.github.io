Numerous instances of the Regional Scrum Gathering®, supported by Scrum Alliance, take place around the Globe every year. South Africa’s event has been running for over ten years. It enjoys a reputation for being an excellent opportunity to learn and to connect with the local Agile and Scrum community; making it a popular, highly anticipated event in the calendar.

In light of our theme for next year we are in search of speakers to present talks and workshops focusing on Connecting agile. If you are interested in being a speaker, please check out our Call for Papers information page to submit your inspirational talks aligned to our theme of Connecting Agile.

All accepted speakers and co-speakers will be eligible for a ticket to attend the full conference.Please ensure that you have familiarised yourself with the Code of Conduct and the Speaker T&Cs before you make a submission.
